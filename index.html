Helping service businesses capture every opportunity

Kellan LLC specializes in SMS automation and CRM setup for plumbing, HVAC, and home service contractors. We help busy business owners capture leads they would otherwise lose due to missed calls, slow follow-ups, and manual processes.

Founded in 2026 and based in Spanish Fork, Utah, we build simple, reliable automation systems that work — so you can focus on running your business instead of managing software.

Our mission: Make professional automation accessible to small businesses without complexity, high costs, or long contracts.
