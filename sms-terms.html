Program Purpose

Kellan operates the "Kellan SMS" program to communicate with customers and prospective customers who have submitted a service or demo request. If you opt in, you may receive text messages related to:

  • Scheduling confirmations and changes
  • Appointment reminders (24 hours and 1 hour before)
  • Technician or team arrival updates
  • Follow-up messages regarding your open service request
  • Occasional promotional messages (only if you opted into marketing texts separately)

How to Opt In

You opt in by checking the SMS consent checkbox on our website contact form at kellanllc.com. The checkbox is unchecked by default. Opting in is voluntary.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the nature and status of your service request. Transactional messages are sent only as relevant to your request.

Cost

Message & data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a single confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. No further messages will be sent. You may re-subscribe at any time by contacting us or re-submitting the form.

Privacy & Data Sharing

Your phone number and SMS opt-in consent are not sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing/promotional use. We do not use purchased or affiliate lead lists for SMS. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

Consent Is Not Required

Consent to receive SMS messages is never a condition of purchase or service. You may receive our services regardless of whether you opt into text messaging.
