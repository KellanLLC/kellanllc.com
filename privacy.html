Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Kellan LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website or submit a request.

Information We Collect

  • Contact info (name, phone number, email, business name) if you submit a form or request a demo.
  • Message content you send us (e.g., notes about what you want automated).
  • Basic technical data (IP address, device/browser, and pages visited) via standard hosting/logs.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To schedule demos and deliver requested services
  • To improve our website and operations

SMS / Text Messaging

If you opt in to receive SMS from Kellan LLC (“Kellan”), we may use your phone number and message content to provide service updates, scheduling, reminders, and customer support communications. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

We may share limited information with service providers that help us deliver messages (e.g., phone carriers, messaging platforms, and automation tools). We do not sell your phone number. SMS opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing/promotional use.

Retention: We retain opt-in records you submit (timestamp, source page, and consent language shown) to support compliance and auditing.

Sharing

We may share information with vendors/contractors that help us operate the website and deliver services, or when required by law.

Cookie & Tracking Practices

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to improve functionality. This may include standard analytics tools such as Google Analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking for third-party ad targeting. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using industry-standard practices.

Your Choices

  • Email: You can request access, updates, or deletion of your info by contacting us.
  • SMS: Reply STOP to opt out of texts at any time. Reply HELP for help.
